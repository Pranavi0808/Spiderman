Spider-Man's adventures are filled with thrilling battles, gripping storylines, and heart-touching moments. As a high-schooler named Peter Parker, he was bitten by a radioactive spider, which granted him superhuman abilities such as superior strength, the ability to cling to walls, and a 'spider-sense' that alerts him to danger. He uses these powers to fight crime in New York City, whilst juggling his personal life and school commitments. He has battled numerous villains such as Green Goblin, Doctor Octopus, and Venom, always striving to uphold his principle of 'With great power, comes great responsibility.' Spider-Man's adventures often test his resolve, pulling him into conflicts that challenge not just his physical strengths, but also his moral values. Despite the challenges, Spider-Man always stands up for what is right, making him one of the most beloved characters in the Marvel Universe.
